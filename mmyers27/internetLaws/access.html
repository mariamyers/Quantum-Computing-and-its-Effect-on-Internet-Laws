With the rapid evolution of the new quantum wave, experts believe that an actual quantum computer will finally be built within the 2020s (Reedy 2017). Although they mostly will only be used by big tech companies and governments. It would be years before the general public would have access, if permitted. That is a big risk because in the wrong hands, a strong quantum computer can do more harm than good.


Not only is the strength a factor but money and size as well. The current quantum computers are as tall as a person, sometimes bigger. One of the quantum computers, the D-Wave 2000Q, has a huge pricetag of $15 million.
