The future of Quantum Computing is very unpredicable but many companies are racing to claim "quantum supremacy". Although what we have currently is very small in comparison to the potential Quantum Computers hold.


Some of the future uses of quantum computing has the potential to put cybersecurity, encryption, blockchain, and the internet at risk. Governments and companies are investing in quantum-enabled internet networks, quantum-proof cryptography, and secure quantum communications (Wilkins, 2021).
